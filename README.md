# Privacy Policy for Puppy Love

**Last Updated: July 7, 2026**

Puppy Love ("we," "us," or "our") is dedicated to protecting your privacy and the safety of our canine community. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your data when you use our app.

## 1. Data We Collect
To provide a social experience for you and your dog, we collect the following information:

| Category | Specific Data Collected | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Account Info** | Name, Email, Profile Picture (via Google Sign-In) | Authentication & communication. |
| **Dog Profile** | Name, Breed, Age, Weight, Bio, Photos | Creating matches and community visibility. |
| **Location** | Precise (GPS) and Coarse location | Finding nearby matches and dog-friendly resources. |
| **Communications** | In-app messages and chat history | Facilitating playdates and social interactions. |
| **Technical Data** | IP address, device ID, app logs, analytics | Performance monitoring and security. |
| **Billing** | Purchase history (handled by Google Play) | Managing premium memberships. |

## 2. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third-party providers to power specific app features:
* **Firebase (Google):** Authentication, Database (Firestore), Storage, and Notifications.
* **Google Maps API:** To provide location-based services and map features.
* **Google Play Billing:** To process membership payments securely.

## 3. Data Sharing & Security
* **No Selling:** We **never** sell your personal data to third parties or advertisers.
* **Visibility:** Your dog's profile and your name are visible to other users to facilitate matching.
* **Security:** We use industry-standard encryption and Firebase security rules to protect your data.

## 4. Your Rights & Data Control
* **Access & Edit:** You can update your profile and dog's info directly in the app.
* **Location Control:** You can enable or disable location permissions at any time via system settings.
* **Data Deletion:** You can delete some or all of your data directly within the app, including:
  * Individual photos and profile information
  * Chat messages and communication history
  * Your dog's profile and associated data
* **Account Deletion:** You can delete your entire account and all associated data (profiles, photos, chats) from the Profile screen. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Puppy Love is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
